Flex

Flex connects yoga instructors and yoga studios to connect open classes to certified instructors. Yoga studios can post available class openings they have and yoga instructors can directly apply to those classes with the click of the button. I made this app a solution for part-time yoga instructors to identify open classes at other studios in the area.

Weekly

Weekly is an open dated planner that allows users to track their weekly tasks and events. I created this app as an electronic replacement for paper planners to allow users the flexibility to plan their week as they desire.

1hike2beer

1hike2beer integrates 3rd party APIs to allow users to simultaneously search for hikes and breweries in the same area. This app was inspired by going on hikes with friends and wanting to plan in advance to find breweries that would be open after so we could all enjoy some beers together.
